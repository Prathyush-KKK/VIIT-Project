The initial step to make n sell an nft is to realize what an NFT is.

These non-fungible tokens have taken the world by storm, but they're not as complicated as you may believe.

Fundamentally, NFTs are a way of registering a digital file on a cryptocurrency blockchain to ensure it is a one-of-a-kind item and show ownership.
